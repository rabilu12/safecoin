Notifications

All applications and requests for funding or sponsorship through charityland.org must be review and evaluate by one or more of our certified agents around the world. Certified agent has the ability to reject or recommend your application for final approval base on the validity of the information you provided. Agents may also request for physical meeting with the beneficiaries before disposal.

Who can become an agent?

Any body who can read and write can become an agent irrespective of his/her country of residence. agents must be willing to undergo a 3-days online training on how to carryout their roles.

How to become an agent?

Application for an agent is currently opened, and will be closed without further notice, once we received the maximum number of applicants we need.
